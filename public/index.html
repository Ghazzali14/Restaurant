About

One of the oldest fine dining restaurants in Chennai specializing in Oriental cuisine. Venture Restaurant, Chennai is ideal for big groups as the quantity served here is humongous, making it ideal for family outings.

We serve a variety of exotic drinks which are the best to accompany the Oriental spread of sushi, fried rice, noodles, and much more. The food is outstanding and the ambiance of the restaurant is a blend of traditional and modernity. The seating arrangement of the restaurant is spread across the indoor seating area which is cozy and comfortable.

Hygiene

Our restaurant is committed to providing a hygienic way of cooking and serving food. We prioritize the cleanliness and safety of our kitchen and dining areas to ensure a healthy dining experience for our valued customers. In our kitchen, we strictly adhere to food safety protocols and maintain high hygiene standards. Our skilled chefs and kitchen staff follow rigorous practices, including proper handwashing, wearing clean uniforms, and using gloves and hairnets. We place great importance on the quality and freshness of our ingredients, ensuring that they are stored, handled, and prepared with utmost care. When it comes to serving our customers, we take every precaution to maintain a hygienic environment. Our dining areas are regularly cleaned and sanitized, with tables, chairs, and other surfaces disinfected between guests. We have implemented measures to ensure proper social distancing and provide a comfortable and safe space for everyone.
